# StockTrack Pricing Guide (Per Property Model)

## 1. Core Pricing Decision

### Base Unit Price
- **ZAR 2,100 per property per month**
- Each property includes:
  - **3 users**
  - **3 storerooms**
- Billing options:
  - Monthly: standard
  - Annual: 10% to 15% discount (recommended max 15%)

### Positioning Statement
StockTrack pricing scales with operational footprint. As clients add properties, they pay for additional value while keeping pricing simple and predictable.

---

## 2. What Is Included Per Property (ZAR 2,100)

- Role-based access (Admin, Manager, Staff)
- Stock visibility and low-stock tracking
- Requisition workflow, including unlisted/procurement lines
- Requisition approvals and review notes
- Controlled issuing against approved requisitions
- Partial issuing with shortfall and restock-needed reporting
- Issuance log and history
- Basic reconciliation workflow
- Standard support (business hours)

---

## 3. Overage and Add-On Rules

### Per Property Inclusions
- 3 users included
- 3 storerooms included

### Add-On Charges
- Additional user (above 3 per property): **ZAR 120/user/month**
- Additional storeroom (above 3 per property): **ZAR 250/storeroom/month**
- Additional onboarding/training session: **ZAR 3,500/session**
- Priority support SLA: **ZAR 1,500/month**

### Commercial Rule
When limits are exceeded, add overage charges. Do not reduce base property price to absorb overages.

---

## 4. Quick Quote Formula

Monthly total =
- (Number of properties x ZAR 2,100)
- + (Extra users x ZAR 120)
- + (Extra storerooms x ZAR 250)
- + Optional add-ons

Example:
- 4 properties
- 5 extra users total
- 2 extra storerooms total

Monthly total:
- (4 x 2,100) + (5 x 120) + (2 x 250)
- = 8,400 + 600 + 500
- = **ZAR 9,500/month**

---

## 5. How This Supports Your ZAR 6,000 Target

If your goal is around ZAR 6,000/month, this model gets there naturally:
- 3 properties = **ZAR 6,300/month** (before overages)

Use this as your anchor:
- Small client (1 to 2 properties): below ZAR 6,000
- Growth client (3+ properties): around or above ZAR 6,000

---

## 6. Objection Handling (Ready Answers)

## Objection 1: Why per property pricing?
Suggested response:
Because value is delivered at property level: stock control, approvals, issuing discipline, and accountability all happen per site.

## Objection 2: We need more users but budget is fixed
Suggested response:
We can start with included users per property and add only the additional users you need now, then scale monthly as the team grows.

## Objection 3: Can you reduce the property fee?
Suggested response:
We keep the property fee fixed for fairness and consistency. The best savings path is annual billing and phased rollout by property.

---
